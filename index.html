campus life

garden high school,kolkata

Garden High School is a private, English-medium, co-educational school in Kasba, Kolkata, West Bengal, India. It was established in 2000 by the Satikanta Guha Foundation.It is a Indian Certificate of Secondary Education (ICSE) school.There are seperate two buildings.One for the junior classes(nursery-1 to class-2) and another for senior classes(class-3 to class-12).They are very much focussed on language development and logical thinking at a very early stage and ensure children to start their first steps into the education system with well-equiped infrastructure.

Library

Garden High School has a humongous library equiped with all types of books.There is a seperate period of library once in a week where students go and read books calmly with silence.Students are also allowed to borrow books from the library and take it home to read for the rest of the week.The library is fully air-conditioned and fully cleaned also.The books of the library are divided into seperate sections like author,funny,detective,fictional,e.t.c.There is very few such huge library available in the schools of kolkata euiped with different types of books.

different types of labs

Garden High School has different types of labs available in the school like physics lab,chemistry lab,biology lab,e.t.c.There students go and see teachers and seniors doing experiments and reactions.There they get an live experience of experimenting things under the observation of teachers.This makes the students to remember those things for a long time.By doing this,students enjoy the thing and at the same time students remember it for a long time.In short,Garden High School offers practical way of learning which help the students to remember those things for lifelong.

Sports & Games
Sports & Games

Students in Garden High School is divided into four houses namely Chinar house,Cedar house,Oak house and Palm house.Inter-house competitions are conducted to promote healthy competition.All the students are interested for the competition and want to make their houses first.Thus,all the students take part in all events energetically.There is a Sports Day once in a year that again make all the students participate for various sports activities like marching,relay-race,drills,e.t.c. where all the parents are invited to see their child's performance.

what's new

  • AV rooms

    Garden High School is newly equipped with AV rooms.The full form of AV rooms is Audio Visual Room.By seeing the full form only we can understand that the rooms have a large lcd screen where we see and there is audio as well.There students are being taken to understand their studies practically in an interesting way.After finishing a chapter,the teacher of the consecutive subjects take the sudents of that class to the av room.There the teacher shows the students a type of movie which is about everything that had been done in the class of that chapter in a form of story telling.The av rooms are fully air-conditioned and is maintained by the technical team.There is very few such AV rooms availble in the schools of Kolkata.
